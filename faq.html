Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GigaHive Private Limited

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or sign in, provide shipping details, and complete payment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery (COD) for eligible orders.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) and overnight shipping (next day) are also available. Processing time is typically 1-2 business days.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package through your account or using the tracking link provided.

What is your return policy?

You can return most items within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange, provided they are unused and in original condition with packaging. See our Returns Policy for details.

How do I return an item?

Log into your account, go to "My Orders", select the item to return, and follow the return process. We'll provide a return shipping label and instructions.

How long does a refund take?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The refund will appear in your account within 3-5 business days after processing.

What if I receive a damaged or wrong item?

Contact us immediately within 48 hours of delivery. Provide photos of the issue, and we'll arrange for a replacement or full refund with free return shipping.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping may be available in the future.

Can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order before it ships. Once shipped, you'll need to return the item following our return policy.

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign In" in the top right corner, then select "Create Account". Fill in your details and verify your email address.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
