Returns & Refunds Policy

Our hassle-free return and refund process

Return Policy

At GigaHive Private Limited, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. You can return most items within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Eligible Items for Return

Items must meet the following criteria:

  • Unused and in original condition
  • In original packaging with all tags and accessories
  • Include original invoice or proof of purchase
  • Not damaged or altered in any way

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Personalized or customized products
  • Perishable goods
  • Intimate or sanitary goods
  • Items damaged by misuse or normal wear
  • Digital products or software

How to Return

  1. Log into your account and go to "My Orders"
  2. Select the item you want to return
  3. Click "Return Item" and fill out the return form
  4. Print the return shipping label
  5. Package the item securely with original packaging
  6. Drop off at the nearest courier service or schedule a pickup

Return Shipping

Return shipping costs:

  • Defective or wrong items: Free return shipping
  • Change of mind: Customer pays return shipping
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping method

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refund will be issued to the original payment method
  • You will receive an email confirmation once refund is processed
  • Bank processing times may vary (typically 3-5 business days)

Exchange Policy

We offer exchanges for:

  • Different size (if available)
  • Different color (if available)
  • Different product (price difference will be adjusted)

Exchanges are subject to product availability.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage or defect
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund
  • Free return shipping for defective items

Questions?

If you have any questions about returns or refunds, please contact our customer support.
