Shipping Information

Everything you need to know about shipping and delivery

Shipping Methods

We offer multiple shipping options to ensure your order arrives safely and on time:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day (available for select locations)

Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are calculated based on:

  • Product weight and dimensions
  • Delivery location
  • Selected shipping method

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. During peak seasons or sales events, processing may take 3-5 business days.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package using:

  • Email confirmation with tracking link
  • Your account order history
  • Our tracking page

Delivery Locations

We currently ship to all major cities and towns across India. For remote locations, delivery may take additional time.

Delivery Issues

If you experience any issues with delivery:

  • Contact our customer support immediately
  • Provide your order number and tracking information
  • We will investigate and resolve the issue promptly

International Shipping

Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping options may be available in the future.
